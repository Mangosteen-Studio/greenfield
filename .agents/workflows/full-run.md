# Full AccountHack Run

## Steps

1. Read and internalize the guardrails:
   - `guardrails/QUALITY_GATES.md` — stage checkpoints to enforce
   - `guardrails/VOICE.md` — output quality rules
   - `guardrails/VERIFICATION.md` — verification discipline

2. Open `ACCOUNT_HACK.md` — this is the canonical workflow.

3. Follow `ACCOUNT_HACK.md` stage by stage, in order:
   - Collect inputs
   - LinkedIn CSV decision
   - Pre-Stage (company context qualifier)
   - Stage 0.5 (Trigger Radar)
   - Stage 1 (Internal Intelligence)
   - Stage 2 (Company Website & Product)
   - Stage 3 (Hiring Signals)
   - Stage 4 (Market & Financial)
   - Stage 5 (Leadership Deep Dive)
   - Stage 5.5 (Executive Narrative & Access)
   - Stage 6 (LinkedIn & Change Agents)
   - Stage 7 (Warm Path Mapping)
   - Stage 8 (Competitive Intelligence)
   - Stage 9 (Industry Language)
   - Stage 10 (Persona Messaging)
   - Final Output (Account War Room)

4. At every stage transition, run the relevant checkpoint from `QUALITY_GATES.md`. Do not skip checkpoints.

5. Build the War Room live as the AE answers. Do not wait until the end.

6. After the War Room is complete, run the War Room Final Validation from `QUALITY_GATES.md`.

7. Output the Completion Status Protocol.

8. Offer to run `/account-review` for a quality audit of the completed War Room.
